Who We Are

AMW Diagnostic Centre —
Built on Trust & Science

AMW Diagnostic Centre (ADC) is a state-of-the-art diagnostic facility located in Islamabad, Pakistan. Established with a commitment to precision medicine, we serve patients, researchers, clinicians, and institutions with the highest standards of laboratory testing.

Our team of qualified scientists and medical technologists operates cutting-edge analytical instruments, ensuring every result is accurate, reliable, and delivered on time.

🎯
Mission
To deliver accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic services that empower healthcare decisions across Punjab.
🔭
Vision
To become the most trusted diagnostic centre in the region through innovation, quality, and compassionate care.
⭐
Quality Policy
We follow strict SOPs and quality control protocols, with all results reviewed by qualified scientific staff.
